Inner Workings

The architecture of empathy: From Academic Research to Product.

Abstract: 2025-11-01

A Novel Multi-Agent Matching System

We propose AI Hive, a multi-agent system that builds a persistent per-user profile by diarizing, transcribing, and analyzing voice sessions. Unlike generic bots, each Hive maintains a structured graph of a user's goals and an embedding vector representing semantic intent.

  • Privacy Preserving PIPEDA/PHIPA compliant with differential privacy.
  • Persistent Memory Long-term models of users, not just session summaries.
  • Fairness Audit Engine Prevents bias in matching recommendations.

The Ambiguity of Experience

"Is it really fair to treat an organization and humans as an entity? Organizations have internal conflict... dominant thoughts float to the top."

We question what makes us human. If intelligence is solved by AI, the human contribution must be the lived experience and the instinctual "gut feeling" that rational systems miss.

Heuristics & Biases

Based on "Judgment under Uncertainty" (Tversky/Kahneman).

AI systems inherit our heuristics (Representativeness, Availability). Without the "missing information" of context, AI makes improper inferences—like misinterpreting mortality statistics for minority groups. Open Up provides the context to correct these biases.

Mere Exposure & Addiction

The Problem: Technologies supporting social interactions are used by anxiously attached people to seek reassurance, creating negative feedback loops.

The Vision: Remove people from the addiction cycle. Facilitate human-to-human interaction with positive intention, rather than seeking external validation to fill an internal void.
